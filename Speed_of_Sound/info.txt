Experimental data for measuring the following parameters.

1)Speed of sound, (m/s)
2) Acoustic Impedance (Kg/m2 s)
3) Attenuation (dB/cm/MHz)


Details of the tissue mimicking Phantom used for experiment:

Cylindrical phantom was used for the experiment with following characteristics
1. Height of phantom (=9.1 mm)  [direction of ultrasound propagation]
2. Radius of phantom = 15 mm or 1.5 cm
3. Mass of phantom= 6.52 grams


Experimental acquisition parameters are as follows:

1. Sampling frequency=100 MHz
2. Transmit frequency= 5 MHz
Remaining parameters are available in the excel data file.
